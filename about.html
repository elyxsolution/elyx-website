About Elyx

Three college grads building digital value — not just websites.

Elyx Solutions is run by three friends from college. We design, build, ship and maintain digital products end to end — development, web, apps, SaaS, marketing and SEO — all of it done by us, no outsourcing.

Work with us See our work

We're three college graduates who started Elyx because we'd rather build great digital work ourselves than watch it get lost between agencies. So we do all of it — in-house.

Between the three of us we cover everything: development, web and app builds, SaaS platforms, marketing and SEO. The people you meet on the first call are the same people who design your screens, write your code and run your campaigns — no account managers, no handoffs, no outsourcing.

That's the whole point of staying small. Decisions happen fast, quality stays consistent, and the same three people are accountable for the outcome from first sketch to the latest deploy.

We're young, hungry and hands-on. We take on a handful of projects at a time so each one gets our full attention, and we stick around long after launch because we believe digital work compounds in value over time.

Beyond just presence, we build digital value — and we measure ourselves by what that value does for the people we work with.
