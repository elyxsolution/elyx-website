Before you ask

Common questions, honest answers.

Everything we get asked before a project kicks off — and a few things you should ask even if you haven't yet.

What makes Elyx different from other web agencies?

Most agencies hand your project to a rotating cast of sub-contractors. At Elyx, a small core team owns every engagement from discovery to launch — same people, same context, start to finish. You get direct access, faster decisions, and work that's actually cohesive.

How much does a project cost?

Engagements start at ₹9,999 for a focused landing page or redesign. Larger platforms and full-stack builds are scoped after a discovery call — we price by outcome, not by the hour. No surprise line items mid-project.

How long does it take to build a website?

A marketing site or landing page typically ships in 1–2 weeks. A complex web app or e-commerce platform takes 4–8 weeks depending on scope. We always agree on a timeline before we start and flag blockers early — no radio silence.

Do you work with clients who already have a website?

Yes — redesigns and migrations are a big part of what we do. We'll audit what you have, keep anything worth keeping (SEO equity, existing content), and rebuild the rest properly. Ashirwad Dental Clinic is a good example of this.

What technologies do you build with?

We're stack-agnostic and pick what fits the job. React, Next.js, and TypeScript for most frontends. Node.js, FastAPI, or Python backends. PostgreSQL or MongoDB for data. We don't lock you into proprietary systems that need us to maintain them forever.

Will I be able to update the site myself after launch?

Yes — we build for handoff. Where it makes sense we integrate a CMS so your team can edit content without touching code. We also document everything and offer a walkthrough session so you're never dependent on us for routine updates.

Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

Yes. We have monthly maintenance and care retainers that cover uptime monitoring, security patches, performance checks, and a set of update hours each month. You can pick this up at launch or add it later — no pressure either way.

What does the process look like from first message to go-live?

A short discovery call → scoped proposal with timeline and price → design (wireframes + visual) → build with two rounds of feedback → QA + staging review → launch. You're looped in at every stage, not just the beginning and end.

Do you only take on large projects, or can smaller businesses work with you?

Small businesses are some of our favourite clients. A focused, well-built website for a local dental clinic or a coaching centre can genuinely change their business. We take on a limited number of projects at a time precisely so smaller engagements get the same attention as bigger ones.

How do payments and contracts work?

50% upfront to begin, 50% on final delivery. Everything is covered in a straightforward statement of work — scope, deliverables, timeline, and revision rounds. No retainer lock-ins unless you want one.
